Communication Joy

Posted on 2016-02-02

I had a long chat with my old friend Feifei last night. The conversation left some subustancial joy inside me that I would like to speak out.

I had problems getting in touch with friends. I want to speak but just do not feel anyone in the world is the person that I can speak to. I feel bad about myself and do not want to contact with anyone who is living better than me in my view. Since my old friends are all quite successfully in their life and career, I nearly disconnect all of them and have no friend to talk with at all. I can just survive in my own world.

I did not contact with Feifei for two years, although we were so close friends fighting all the way together. From undergraduate, we were in the same major and both of us take the same minor - English. We had lots of time going to classes and study together. The dream to apply aboard starts in our senior year of college and we begun to prepare for TOFEL exams together in the hot summer 2006. We went to Beijing to attend Xin Dong Fang GRE during the spring festival 2006. That month was special memory to me because of all the hardness. We took the GRE exam and later on I went to Shenzhen. After a year, we both got accepted and went to North America. The begining term is hard and we both encouraged each other. I went Davis, California to visit Feifei in the summer of 2010. Later on, both of us met difficulties and problems in our study and life. Feifei is a person I respected, she is humber, hardworking and the one who bear the pain silently.

I did not contact her for two years, but kept thinking of her a lot and prey for her. After so many years struggle, we both kind of loose our hands about ourselves and life. Maybe do not dream high, you can lead a life at least you can survive not suffocate by yourself.

Thinking of my old friends and wish you all the best in your life !
